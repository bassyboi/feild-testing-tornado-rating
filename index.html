Supercell Wind Shear Chart

About This Chart

This chart estimates the bulk wind shear (in s⁻¹) across a supercell assuming a default storm “height” (vertical separation) of 6000 m.

The updraft speed is estimated from CAPE (available energy) using:
w = √(2 × CAPE)

The effective advective wind that tilts the storm is:
Uₑff = w × tan(θ)

and the bulk wind shear is approximated as:
S = (2 × Uₑff) / 6000

In this chart we assume that a storm tilt between 10° and 20° is in the danger zone for tornado development.
